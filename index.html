Privacy Policy for Songs of Eternal Life

Last Updated: July 3, 2025

Thank you for using Songs of Eternal Life. This privacy policy explains what data the application stores and how it is handled.

Data Storage

Songs of Eternal Life stores the following information directly and exclusively on your device:

  • Your list of favorite songs.
  • Your history of recently viewed songs.
  • Any custom playlists you create.
  • Your preferred settings, such as font size and dark mode preference.

This data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS features (UserDefaults) to enhance your user experience and provide personalization.

Data Collection and Transmission

Songs of Eternal Life does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data or usage information. We do not have access to your favorites, recents, playlists, or any other information from your device. All data remains on your device and is under your control.
